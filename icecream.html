Ice Cream

We make all of our ice cream by hand, in small batches, on 18th Street. In the bakery adjoining our ice cream kitchen we make everything that goes into our ice cream, whether it’s brownies, peanut brittle, marshmallows, or the snickerdoodles for ricanelas! We have lots of homemade toppings to jazz up your ice cream including almond toffee, graham crackers, and spiced pecans to name just a few of the most popular (and don’t forget to ask for our decadent homemade hot fudge to top it off!)

Straus Family Creamery, only 45 miles away, delivers all of the organic milk, cream and eggs for our recipes. Our salted caramel, roasted banana, honey lavender, and other flavors draw lines that wrap around the block!
